The Greatest Shot in NBA History

The 1997 NBA Finals was a classic battle. Led by Michael Jordan and Scottie Pippen, the Bulls faced a tough challenge from Karl Malone and John Stockton. One of the most iconic Game 5, known as the “Flu Game,” where Michael Jordan, visibly ill and physically drained, delivered a heroic 38-point performance to lead his team to victory. Where in my opinion this was the greatest shot in history.
